How much would your fans pay to see you in person?

It’s up to you. You know your fans best—price your scans accordingly.

Typical monetization models:

  • Freemium Model – Share teaser scans for free; the full content is behind a paywall.
  • Premium Model – All scans are behind a paywall.
  • Add-ons Model – Fans pay a set price per scan.

Pro Tip: Take a preview photo during scan creation.

FAQ

Where are my scans stored?
On U.S. cloud storage, with access granted via QR code or link.

Who can access my scans?
Only those with your QR code or link.

Do I own my scans?
Yes. You retain ownership. OnlyScans can distribute your content for services.

Can I delete my scans?
Yes. Deleting them removes them from your device and our storage.

Can scans be saved/distributed?
They're protected, but sharing of QR codes or links can't be fully prevented.
