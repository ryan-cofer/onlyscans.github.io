Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 19, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app.
1. Information We Collect
a. Information You Provide
Account Information: By signing into the app using Sign In With Apple, we are provided with your unique Apple User Identifier.
User Generated Content: Any scans, links, images or other content that you share within the app.
Contact Information: We may collect and store any contact information you provide.

b. Information We Collect Automatically
Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (e.g., usage, usage frequency, usage behavior, viewed or uploaded scans).
Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers, and crash reports.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app
- Enable content sharing and viewing
- Personalize your experience
- Communicate with you including for support and updates
- Monitor and analyze usage and trends
- Protect against abuse or violation of our Terms
3. How We Share Your Information
Your information may be shared with:
- Other users: Content you share may be shared with other users
- Service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate the app, such as hosting or analytics providers
- Legal authorities: If required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public
- Business transfer parties: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that process
4. Content Visibility and Control
Content creation: Content that you create but don't share remains local to your device and is unavailable to OnlyScans. Content that you create and share will be visible to OnlyScans and by those who have access to the associated QR code or Link.

Editing or deleting content: You are able to delete scans that you have created, whether they have or haven't been shared.
5. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please use the app responsibly.
6. Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for children under the age of 17 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
7. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or other means. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at onlyscansapp@gmail.com.
